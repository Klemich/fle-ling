Voici differents commentaires google-maps postés dans les écoles où j'ai travaillé

I did an intensive course for 3 weeks. I had 3 different teachers during the time because I switched level during the course of the weeks. I liked each individual teacher for their different qualities and appreciated their teaching a lot. I found their levels of knowledge proficient but was especially impressed by Clément who taught the A2 level. His detailed description of many rules and structures of the language really helped and will be a good guide as I continue learning the language on my own. They offered excursions too, which is a great initiative and lots of fun - chocolaterie and museum visits. I'm really happy with the school and the course. Great learning atmosphere and nice people. Something which could be improved is the assessment of the entry test. I ended up in a group which I believe was too advanced for me but did not know if the lower level would be better or too low. I wish I had had more guidance in this beyond the entry test I did for the application. I do think a level in between the two would have been better for me but getting some thorough reviews of some basics really helped me. It was fine to change groups from one week to another, which I'm happy about. I also think the application process was rather complicated. The communication between Language International and CEBP was rather slow, and late in the process I was told that the week I had chosen to start was an exam week for the previous period so I should start a week later. This worked out fine but the information given, that it's possible to start any Monday during the summer, was very misleading. Other than that, I'm very happy with my experience.
